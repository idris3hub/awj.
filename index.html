About Us

AWJ is a leading company in the fields of aircraft services and catering support, whether in the military or civil sectors in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and the Gulf countries. It provides comprehensive and integrated solutions to its customers, starting from periodic maintenance to the provision of spare parts and inventory management for aircraft and their supporting equipment (ground equipment) and fire and rescue equipment. The company focuses on achieving the highest safety standards. The company has qualified technical and administrative cadres to carry out any work related to the field, as it relies on the latest international technologies to ensure the quality of work in addition to an attractive, comfortable and encouraging work environment for its employees.
